Leading without authority

What does it mean to lead without authority?

There are often times when a group must be led without anyone assigning (formal) authority to you. What are the right ways to help lead or guide that group through a process or to make a decision. Often, as a Business Analyst, you should be treated as a trusted advisor, whose opinion should be respected. However, you may not be treated as such.
